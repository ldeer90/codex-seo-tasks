## Example Copy

### Page Title

Midi Dresses - Formal & Occasion Midis - Melani the Label

### Meta Description

Shop Australian-made midi dresses by Melani the Label, with stretch, lined and occasion-ready styles for formal events, parties and evenings out.

### H1

MIDI DRESSES

### Hero Subheading

FORMAL, PARTY & EVENING MIDIS

### H2

MIDI DRESSES FOR FORMAL AND OCCASION STYLE

Melani the Label midi dresses are designed for the moments that call for shape, polish and ease in one piece. The collection brings together Australian-made midi dress styles with stretch fabrication, lined finishes, gathered detail and considered necklines, giving you a refined option for formal events, birthday celebrations, evening plans and dressier nights out. Choose from sculpted silhouettes, lace finishes and colour-led styles that feel elevated without needing complicated styling.

### H3

FORMAL AND PARTY MIDI DRESS SILHOUETTES

For a formal midi dress, look for clean lines, supportive stretch and details that hold their shape through the night. Black, white and red midi dresses can each shift the mood of the outfit, while lace midi dress options add texture for engagements, bridal showers and special occasions. If you want more length or a different event edit, explore the formal dresses and bridal dresses collections for related silhouettes.

### H3

HOW TO STYLE THE MELANI MIDI DRESS RANGE

A midi dress works best when the proportions feel intentional. Pair high neck or sleeve-focused styles with simple heels and minimal jewellery, or keep a fitted party midi dress sharp with a small bag and clean hair. For shoppers comparing different cuts, the all dresses collection is the easiest place to see the wider range, while long sleeve dresses and lace dresses are useful next steps when coverage or fabric detail matters.
