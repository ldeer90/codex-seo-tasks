## Example Copy

### Page Title

Office Catering & Lunch Delivery - Salad Servers Direct

### Meta Description

Fresh office catering made easy with ready-made salads, platters and sides delivered for team lunches, meetings and workplace events across NSW, VIC, QLD and ACT.

### H1

OFFICE CATERING

### Hero Subheading

SALADS, LUNCHES & WORKPLACE EVENTS

### H2

NEED EASY OFFICE CATERING?

Whether you are planning a team lunch, client meeting, training day or office celebration, Salad Servers Direct makes office catering simple. Our ready-made salads, pasta salads, slaws and sides arrive prepared, generous and easy to serve, so you can feed the team without organising a full-service caterer. Choose fresh favourites for everyday lunches, lighter options for working meetings, or bulk salads for larger workplace events, with delivery available across selected areas in NSW, VIC, QLD and ACT.

### H2

OFFICE CATERING FOR TEAM LUNCHES

Good office lunch catering should feel easy for the person ordering and enjoyable for everyone eating. Our ready-made salads are designed for sharing, with generous portions that work well for staff lunches, department catch-ups and casual Friday meals. Choose a mix of classics, pasta salads and lighter options so people can build a plate that suits them, without a long prep list or messy kitchen handover.

Salad Suggestions: Classic Caesar Salad 2kg, Pesto Pasta Salad 2.5kg, Australian Potato Salad 2.5kg.

### H2

OFFICE MEETING CATERING FOR BOARDROOMS

For boardroom meetings, client presentations and working lunches, the food needs to be fresh, tidy and simple to serve. Salad Servers Direct gives you office meeting catering that looks polished on the table without needing staff, setup or complicated serving. Pair a few substantial salads with lighter grain and leafy options so guests can eat comfortably while the day keeps moving.

Salad Suggestions: Lentil Salad with Extra Virgin Olive Oil 2.5kg, Kale & Quinoa with Tahini Salad 2.5kg, Basil Penne with Roasted Pumpkin & Spinach Salad 2.5kg.

### H2

CORPORATE CATERING FOR WORKPLACE EVENTS

From training days to end-of-quarter lunches, larger workplace events call for food that can handle a crowd. Our bulk salads and sides make corporate catering feel more relaxed, with colourful options that work alongside mains, platters or a simple self-serve lunch table. Choose a mix of hearty salads, fresh greens and plant-based sides to give the team plenty of variety.

Salad Suggestions: Greek Salad Kit 2.4kg, Black Rice, Beetroot, Walnut and Feta Salad 2.5kg, Herby Quinoa & Broccoli Salad with Green Tahini Dressing 2.5kg.

### H2

FRESH AND HEALTHY OFFICE CATERING OPTIONS

When you want a fresher office catering option, salads are an easy way to bring colour and balance to the table. Build your order around crisp leafy salads, Mediterranean-style favourites, grains, pulses and plant-based options. It is a simple way to cater for different tastes while keeping the meal light, generous and workplace-friendly.

Salad Suggestions: Garden Salad 1kg, Mediterranean Chickpea Salad 2.5kg, Edamame, Freekeh & Kale Salad 2.5kg.

### H2

OFFICE PARTY CATERING MADE SIMPLE

Office parties, milestone lunches and staff celebrations do not need to be complicated. Add a few crowd-pleasing salads to the table and you have an easy spread that works with barbecue, roast meats, sandwiches, wraps or other shared mains. Our office party catering options are made for serving straight from the pack, giving you less to organise before everyone arrives.

Salad Suggestions: Creamy Coleslaw Salad 2.5kg, Thai Beef Noodle Salad 2.5kg, Chicken Katsu Salad Leafy Kit 1.6kg.
