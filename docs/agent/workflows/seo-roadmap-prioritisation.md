# SEO Roadmap Prioritisation

Turn audits, rankings, GSC, content gaps, and business context into a practical SEO roadmap.

## Use When

- The user asks for a roadmap, strategy, priority plan, next actions, or what to do after audits/research.

## Required Inputs

- Client sidecar and brief.
- Recent audits/reports/research exports where available.
- Business constraints, campaign priorities, or service/product priorities.

## Phase 0 - Access And Input Preflight

- Memory: follow `docs/agent/client-memory.md`; read the client brief, sidecar when present, and timeline before workflow-specific sources, then append the client timeline with the proof summary or blocker.
- Confirm client sidecar/brief and gather the newest available evidence before creating roadmap items.
- Confirm Drive/Monday destination if the roadmap will be written or tasked.
- Confirm any business priorities that cannot be inferred from client docs or recent deliverables.

### Missing-input routing

Route missing client setup to `ld-seo-client-onboarding`, access/Drive/Monday blockers to `ld-seo-maintenance`, missing collection evidence to `ld-seo-collection-seo`, missing content evidence to `ld-seo-content-briefs`, and missing GSC evidence to `gsc-opportunity-mining.md`.

## Process

1. Validate the client sidecar.
2. Gather the latest available evidence: GA4, GSC, SE Ranking, audits, content briefs, metadata sheets, and Monday status.
3. Identify candidate actions across technical SEO, metadata, content, internal linking, collection/category expansion, tracking, and reporting.
4. Use Codex judgement to prioritise by impact, effort, confidence, dependency, and business value.
5. Create a client-ready roadmap Doc or Sheet with priority, rationale, owner, recommended timing, and proof source.
6. Create or update Monday tasks only after board schema and client context are confirmed.

## Quality Gate

- Do not create roadmap items without evidence or business rationale.
- Every recommendation needs a source, expected benefit, and next action.
- Read back the deliverable and any Monday tasks.

## Proof Block

Report evidence sources, recommendations created, Drive folder, deliverable URL, Monday tasks, warnings, open assumptions, and client timeline update status.
