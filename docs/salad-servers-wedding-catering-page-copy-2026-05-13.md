### Page Title

Wedding Salads & Catering Sides Delivered - Salad Servers Direct

### Meta Description

Ready-made wedding salads and sides for relaxed receptions, buffets and backyard celebrations. Explore bulk salads and fresh sides delivered across NSW, VIC, QLD and ACT.

### H1

WEDDING CATERING

### Hero Subheading

SALADS, SIDES & CELEBRATIONS

### H2

PLANNING WEDDING FOOD?

If you are planning a relaxed reception, backyard wedding or shared celebration meal, ready-made wedding salads can make the food feel easier without losing that fresh, generous feeling. Salad Servers Direct offers prepared salads and sides that work beautifully with buffet tables, shared mains and casual wedding lunches. Choose colourful salads, classic sides and guest-friendly options that are simple to serve, so you can spend more time enjoying the day and less time managing the food.

### H2

WEDDING SALADS FOR RECEPTIONS

Wedding salads should look inviting, feel fresh and be easy for guests to enjoy. Build the table with a mix of crisp greens, colourful slaws, pasta salads and Mediterranean-style sides so there is something for different tastes. These salads work well for buffet-style meals, long-table lunches and relaxed receptions where guests can serve themselves.

Salad Suggestions: Greek Salad Kit 2.4kg, Corn & Coriander Slaw Salad 2.5kg, Mediterranean Chickpea Salad 2.5kg.

### H2

BACKYARD WEDDING CATERING SIDES

Backyard weddings are often the most memorable because they feel personal and relaxed. The food should match that feeling. Our ready-made salads and sides are easy to serve alongside barbecue, roast meats, seafood, vegetarian mains or a grazing-style table. They help keep the wedding menu generous without adding more cooking, chopping or last-minute kitchen jobs.

Salad Suggestions: Pesto Pasta Salad 2.5kg, Australian Potato Salad 2.5kg, Garden Salad 1kg.

### H2

SALADS AND SIDES FOR SHARED MAINS

If you already have the mains sorted, salads are one of the easiest ways to complete the wedding menu. Pair classic salads with roast chicken, lamb, seafood, baked fish or vegetarian dishes, then add a few bolder flavours for colour and interest. It is a practical way to make the meal feel abundant without turning the day into a full catering production.

Salad Suggestions: Classic Caesar Salad 2kg, Black Rice, Beetroot, Walnut and Feta Salad 2.5kg, Tabbouleh Salad 2.5kg.

### Mid-Page CTA

SALADS FOR EVERY CELEBRATION

### H2

GUEST-FRIENDLY WEDDING SALAD VARIETY

A good salad for a wedding menu should make guests feel considered. Mix familiar favourites with lighter, plant-based and grain-based options so people can choose what suits them. This is especially helpful for summer wedding salads, outdoor receptions and long lunches where fresh sides can make the whole meal feel brighter.

Salad Suggestions: Herby Quinoa & Broccoli Salad with Green Tahini Dressing 2.5kg, Lentil Salad with Extra Virgin Olive Oil 2.5kg, Spice Roasted Cauliflower Salad with Tahini and Almonds 2.5kg.

### H2

KEEPING WEDDING FOOD SIMPLE

Wedding food does not always need a complicated package or formal service. For many couples, the priority is having enough fresh, good-looking food that is easy to serve and easy for guests to enjoy. Ready-made wedding salads and sides can help keep the menu simple, especially when you are planning a self-managed reception or a celebration at home.

### H2

PRE-WEDDING AND RECOVERY LUNCH IDEAS

Salads and sides can also help around the wedding day itself, from family lunches before the ceremony to next-day recovery meals. Keep a few fresh options on hand for guests staying nearby, bridal party lunches or a casual meal after the celebration. It is a simple way to feed people well while keeping the mood relaxed.

Product Suggestions: Small Salads, Pumpkin Soup 1.5kg, Hot Meals.
