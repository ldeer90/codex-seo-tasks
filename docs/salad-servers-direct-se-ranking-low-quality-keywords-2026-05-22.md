## Main Finding

The project is much cleaner after the 2026-05-15 cleanup, but it still carries avoidable keyword-engine pairs. The strongest removal candidates are terms that now belong in the new Salad Servers Wholesale project, broad meal-delivery terms where Direct is not ranking or the intent is too generic, non-core city terms, and duplicate salad-delivery variants.

## Volume-Adjusted Recheck

After a second pass using 2026-05-22 project stats, the removal list should be more conservative than the first pass. Several terms that look noisy by intent have enough search volume and/or current rank to justify keeping temporarily.

Key adjustments:

- Do not automatically remove city terms with real rank and meaningful volume. `meal delivery canberra` (volume 210, position 9), `meal delivery gold coast` (volume 210, position 6), `salads adelaide` (volume 170, position 7), `salads catering melbourne` (volume 70, position 4), and `soup delivery melbourne` (volume 50, position 1) are worth keeping if Salad Servers wants location visibility reporting.
- Do not delete newly added high-volume meal terms too quickly. `meals delivered` (volume 1500), `meal prep delivery` (volume 590), `diet meals delivered` (volume 590), `prepared meals delivered` (volume 390), and related meal-delivery terms are broad, but they have only had limited time in the project after recent cleanup/additions.
- The easiest cuts are still low-volume duplicates, zero-volume/no-data terms, wholesale terms now covered by the Wholesale project, and terms with weak fit plus no ranking.
